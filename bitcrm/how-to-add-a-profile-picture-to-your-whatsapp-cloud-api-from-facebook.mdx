title: "How to Add a Profile Picture to Your WhatsApp Cloud API from Facebook"
description: "Personalizing your WhatsApp Cloud API profile with a profile picture enhances your brand's identity and builds trust with customers. Follow these steps to upload your profile picture via Facebook Business Manager."
---

import {Step, StepList, Alert} from "@mintlify/components";

## Step 1: Access Phone Number Settings

1. Navigate to [Facebook Business Manager](https://business.facebook.com/wa/manage/phone-numbers/).
2. Select the phone number you wish to update.
3. Click the **Settings** ⚙️ icon next to the phone number.

<img
  src="/images/whatsapp-phone-number-settings.png"
  alt="WhatsApp Phone Number Settings"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 2: Open Profile Settings

1. In the settings menu, go to the **Profile** tab on the left-hand side.
2. Click the **Choose file** button to select your profile picture.

<img
  src="/images/whatsapp-profile-settings.png"
  alt="WhatsApp Profile Settings"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 3: Upload Your Profile Picture

1. Choose an image file for your profile picture.
2. Ensure the image is at least 192px by 192px and is in JPG or PNG format.
3. Wait for the image to upload.

<img
  src="/images/upload-profile-picture.png"
  alt="Upload Profile Picture"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 4: Preview and Save

1. Preview the uploaded image on the right side of the page.
2. If the image appears correctly, click **Save** to update your profile picture.

<img
  src="/images/preview-and-save.png"
  alt="Preview and Save Profile Picture"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 5: Profile Picture Live

Your WhatsApp Cloud API profile picture is now live and visible to your customers.
