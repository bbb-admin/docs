import {Step, StepList, Alert} from "@mintlify/components";

## Step 1: Go to Campaign Dashboard

Navigate to your [bitCRM Campaign dashboard](https://app.bitbybit.studio) and click "Create new campaign."

<img
  src="/images/campaign-dashboard.png"
  alt="Campaign Dashboard"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 2: Select a Channel

Click "WhatsApp Cloud API."

<img
  src="/images/select-channel.png"
  alt="Select Channel"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 3: Set Up Your Campaign

Enter a "Campaign name" and choose your "Segmentation" from the dropdown. For details on creating customer segments, refer to [How to Create a Customer Segment](https://www.bitbybit.studio/knowledge-base/how-to-create-a-customer-segment).

<img
  src="/images/setup-campaign.png"
  alt="Set Up Campaign"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 4: Schedule & Choose Message Template

Click "Send immediately" or "Schedule send" by selecting the date and time. Then choose your campaign template. For details on creating message templates, refer to [How to Create a Message Template Campaign for WhatsApp API](https://www.bitbybit.studio/knowledge-base/how-to-create-a-message-template-campaign-for-whatsapp-api).

Click "Review."

<img
  src="/images/schedule-template.png"
  alt="Schedule & Choose Template"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>
