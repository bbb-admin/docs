---

Dynamic variables personalize your bitCRM automation messages, tailoring communication with customer and order data. This guide outlines how to use dynamic variables to personalize messages regarding orders and customer information:

## ğŸ“¦ Order Data

Use the following dynamic variables to personalize messages regarding orders:

- **Order ID**: `{{order.id}}`
- **Order Name**: `{{order.name}}`
- **Order Total Price**: `{{order.totalPrice}}`
- **Order Note**: `{{order.note}}`
- **Order Item Lines**: `{{order.itemLines}}`
- **Order Properties**: `{{order.itemLines.properties.name[index]}} : {{order.itemLines.properties.value[index]}}`
- **Order Link**: `{{order.link}}`

Incorporate customer details into your messages:

- **Full Name**: `{{order.customer.name}}`
- **First Name**: `{{order.customer.firstName}}`
- **Last Name**: `{{order.customer.lastName}}`
- **Email**: `{{order.customer.email}}`
- **Phone**: `{{order.customer.phone}}`
- **Address**: `{{order.customer.address}}`

Include shipping information for better personalization:

- **Address Line 1**: `{{order.shippingAddress.address1}}`
- **Address Line 2**: `{{order.shippingAddress.address2}}`
- **Country**: `{{order.shippingAddress.country}}`
- **Province**: `{{order.shippingAddress.province}}`

- **ZIP Code**: `{{order.shippingAddress.zip}}`
