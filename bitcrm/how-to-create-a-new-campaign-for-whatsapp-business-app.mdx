## Step 1: Go to Campaign Dashboard

Navigate to your bitCRM Campaign dashboard and click "Create new campaign."

![795a48a5 0b1f 4de6 Bbcd 791504548e9a Pn](/images/795a48a5-0b1f-4de6-bbcd-791504548e9a.png)

## Step 2: Select Campaign Type

Choose "WhatsApp Cloud API" as your campaign type.

![Ad307bf3 3232 4df3 8507 Dfe59e3c5264 Pn](/images/ad307bf3-3232-4df3-8507-dfe59e3c5264.png)

## Step 3: Configure Campaign Details

Fill in the campaign details, including name, schedule, and target audience.

![B52c4f8c F1b1 4f74 9531 Eb1535ffac8d Pn](/images/b52c4f8c-f1b1-4f74-9531-eb1535ffac8d.png)

## Step 4: Add Message Content

Compose your message content and customize it as needed.

![Acf0ed9f 07b2 436d 96ad Daf5a2ef0c2b Pn](/images/acf0ed9f-07b2-436d-96ad-daf5a2ef0c2b.png)

## Step 5: Review and Schedule

Review your campaign settings and click "Schedule" to activate the campaign.

![0fa3e933 5e31 4673 B3eb Ae7a85aafb57 Pn](/images/0fa3e933-5e31-4673-b3eb-ae7a85aafb57.png)
