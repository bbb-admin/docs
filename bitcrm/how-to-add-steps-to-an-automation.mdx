---
title: "How to Add Steps to an Automation"
---

To add steps to an automation in bitCRM, follow this simple guide:

## Step 1: Access the Automation Dashboard

Go to the [bitCRM Automation dashboard](https://app.bitbybit.studio).

## Step 2: Create a New Scenario

Click **"Create new scenario"**.

<img
  src="https://www.bitbybit.studio/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/WhatsApp-API-Automation-Template-Dashboard.png"
  alt="WhatsApp API Automation Template Dashboard"
/>

## Step 3: Define the Trigger

Select a trigger based on your business needs, such as:

- **Shopify Triggers**: Order Fulfilled, Order Created, Abandoned Cart
- **bitLogin Triggers**: User Login, New Sign Up

Click **"+"** to add a step.

<img
  src="https://www.bitbybit.studio/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/add-steps.png"
  alt="Add Steps"
/>

## Step 4: Add Actions

Choose actions for the automation like:

- **Send Message**: Choose the channel and craft your message.
- **Delay Action**: Set a delay before the next step is triggered.
- **Product Review Request**: Ask customers for a product review.

## Step 5: Save the Scenario

Click **"Save"** and then **"Save Automation"**.

<img
  src="https://www.bitbybit.studio/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/save-automation.png"
  alt="Save Automation"
/>
