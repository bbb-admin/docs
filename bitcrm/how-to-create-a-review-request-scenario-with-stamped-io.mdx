title: "How to Create a Review Request Scenario with Stamped.io"
---

Gather product reviews efficiently by connecting Stamped.io with bitbybit integrations. This integration simplifies the review request process, allowing you to collect feedback that enhances your brand's credibility. Here’s how to set it up.

## **Step 1:** Access the [Integrations](https://app.bitbybit.studio/integrations) dashboard and click "connect" Stamped.io.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66ea54e11e90e2706fd715be_AD_4nXdukS2Z62kliFDVDrBdEiVv89ezDkXwCNZWxHfYH0-r-1nwdhmoX1hquhjnSxima3v_5PYZfJqwxn8m7dLN4H1JJzRqsm1t0rY60zEgSWBUlt9HPatgp6iQuG2729pVOHA4Dbs3vZ5jxHT_yb_g2g5wNp8.png)Enter your "API Public Key", "API Private Key", “store hash” then click "Continue"Stamped io will be directly integrated into your Shopify.
