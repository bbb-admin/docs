---
title: "How to Add an Image to Your Campaign"
description: "Incorporating images into your bitCRM campaigns enhances visual appeal and can boost engagement. This guide will show you how to add an image to your campaign template for a more compelling message."
---

import {Step, StepList, Alert} from "@mintlify/components";

## Step 1: Go to the bitCRM Template Dashboard

Navigate to your bitCRM Template dashboard and click **"Create new template"**.

<img
  src="/images/create-new-template.png"
  alt="Create New Template"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 2: Select Campaign Type

Choose **"Campaign"** as the template type.

<img
  src="/images/select-campaign-type.png"
  alt="Select Campaign Type"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 3: Choose WhatsApp Channel

Select between **"WhatsApp Business App"** or **"WhatsApp Cloud API"** as your campaign channel.

<img
  src="/images/select-whatsapp-channel.png"
  alt="Select WhatsApp Channel"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 4: Fill in the Required Template Fields

Complete the necessary fields for your template, including name, language, and message content.

<img
  src="/images/fill-template-fields.png"
  alt="Fill Template Fields"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 5: Upload an Image

Click on **"Upload image"**, select your desired image file, and click **"Save template"**.

<img src="/images/upload-image.png" alt="Upload Image" className="rounded-lg" />

## Step 6: Preview Your Message

On the right sidebar, preview your message to ensure it appears as expected.

<img
  src="/images/preview-message.png"
  alt="Preview Message"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>
