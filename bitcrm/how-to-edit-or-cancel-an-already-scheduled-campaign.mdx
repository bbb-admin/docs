title: "How to Edit or Cancel an Already Scheduled Campaign"
---

Editing or canceling scheduled campaigns in bitCRM gives you the power to adjust your messaging strategy. This tutorial shows you how to manage scheduled campaigns for optimal control over your outreach. Follow these simple steps:

## **Step 1:** Go to [bitCRM Campaign](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitcrm/campaign) Dashboard

## **Step 2:** Click on the "Campaign" to make edits.
