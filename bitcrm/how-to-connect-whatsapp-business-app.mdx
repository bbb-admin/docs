---

import {Step, StepList, Alert} from "@mintlify/components";

## Step 1: Access the Integration Menu

1. Log in to your bitbybit account.
2. Navigate to the **Integrations** menu.

<img
  src="/images/integrations-menu.png"
  alt="bitbybit Integration Menu"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 2: Find WhatsApp Business App

1. Scroll down until you find **WhatsApp Business App**.
2. Click the **Connect** button next to it.

<img
  src="/images/connect-whatsapp.png"
  alt="Connect WhatsApp Business App Button"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 3: A QR Code Will Appear

After clicking **Connect**, a QR code will appear on your screen.

<img
  src="/images/qr-code.png"
  alt="WhatsApp Business App QR Code"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 4: Open WhatsApp on Your Mobile Device

1. Open the **WhatsApp** app on your mobile device.
2. Tap **Menu** or **Settings**, then select **Linked Devices**.

<img
  src="/images/whatsapp-settings.png"
  alt="WhatsApp Menu on Mobile"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 5: Scan the QR Code

1. Tap **Link a Device**.
2. Scan the QR code that appears on your computer screen.
3. After the scan is successful, tap **Continue** to complete the process.

<img
  src="/images/scan-qr.png"
  alt="Scanning QR Code with WhatsApp"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 6: Verify the Integration

Once the process is complete, verify the integration by visiting **LiveChat** in bitChat. If your WhatsApp chat appears on the LiveChat dashboard, the integration is successful.

Congratulations! You have successfully connected WhatsApp Business App with bitbybit. You can now communicate directly with customers through bitCRM and bitChat.
