---
title: "How to Test and Preview Your Automation"
---

Testing and previewing your automation in bitCRM is a crucial step to ensure everything runs smoothly. It allows you to simulate the actions and evaluate the results before deploying your automation to customers. Follow these steps to effectively test and preview your automation.

## Step 1: Access the Automation Dashboard

Go to the [bitCRM Automation dashboard](https://app.bitbybit.studio).

## Step 2: Select the Automation to Test

Find the automation scenario you want to test from the list and click on it.

<img
  src="https://www.bitbybit.studio/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/WhatsApp-API-Automation-Template-Dashboard.png"
  alt="WhatsApp API Automation Template Dashboard"
/>

## Step 3: Test the Scenario

Click **"Test"** to initiate the testing process. This will simulate the actions triggered by your selected conditions.

<img
  src="https://www.bitbybit.studio/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/test-scenario.png"
  alt="Test scenario"
/>

## Step 4: Review the Test Results

Once the test is complete, review the results to ensure that all actions performed as expected. You can check whether the correct messages were sent, if any errors occurred, or if the actions were carried out in the right order.

## Step 5: Preview the Automation

Click **"Preview"** to see how the automation will look in action. This allows you to view how the messages will be delivered to customers.

<img
  src="https://www.bitbybit.studio/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/preview-automation.png"
  alt="Preview automation"
/>

## Step 6: Make Adjustments

If the test results aren't as expected, make the necessary adjustments to your automation scenario. You can modify triggers, actions, or conditions based on the feedback from the test.

## Step 7: Save and Activate

Once you're satisfied with the results, click **"Save"** to save your changes and **"Activate"** to set your automation live.

<img
  src="https://www.bitbybit.studio/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/save-activate-automation.png"
  alt="Save and Activate Automation"
/>
