---
title: "How to Integrate bitCRM with Shopify"
---

Seamlessly integrate bitCRM with Shopify to streamline customer management and orders. By connecting these platforms, you can centralize data and improve customer engagement. Follow these steps for a smooth integration.

## **Step 1: Access Integrations Dashboard**

- Go to the [Integrations](https://app.bitbybit.studio/integrations) dashboard and click "Connect".

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66f236d96507371d838668d3_AD_4nXef3fqzhMtx7gucSUukaAaHSjri_u5iZdn210-72ixv8Ml5paZGog607x2424biTJMt6ZPzXmcCxZvYfvpkcngoLQVyFO_wmVaAW4CYCWaEdRqZGyBKWwxMrXGDdjTmlrk3j5GHEre2vew4G5eU-1IqMuqh.png)

Click "Connect"

## **Step 2: Connect to Shopify**

- Click "Connect to Shopify" and click “Install”.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66f236d9468f4c81409b7215_AD_4nXeReCARmi7IFPivPzpODuusCA8wfEfMgDqCenfpkvjJVb7Q5HeWYNuVN9gvjNEz0V-5XPMvHQMHHNEFPVWZALP-EFGqIo2MHtqSgm0ZgRdVSYz3tmyu4xDWb8ZBfBZff4g8qU753jA4M2fwrzmJTbMvs5Se.png)

Click "Connect to Shopify"

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66f236d93ddda8c3c98350c1_AD_4nXdtbczJqZQ5igzC0gcUowLG4LQlR7U9VTTLYRDAmea7QtVI5fkpqQapVJ3v_4pyUm8KnTUtpTAhnBpAtzBLWzfvahRAR822gk_2vEmARyhcX1OWXjSdtlRsEdGDwOAJ0ksYKSL7dTDJrY0sSd_3q3-qrZ_x.png)

Click “Install”

## **Step 3: Choose Your Shopify Store**

- Select the company or "Create new company" or "Login to another account".

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66f236d96507371d838668ce_AD_4nXdWGvBf44L0zmvCu42ge5Td_DUJklngybStDS9qbIPdaT_TwSJ4Siqg92O7IO4EXiWdkuLG1XhJ-uRxOvqK95Mk4ZKQ-KFwv8qgr2BKq8UNTTkVb1GIY8IHkMjXCVGtkLrYpfRVHjimra9_KzngVOhkY1I1.png)

Select the company or "Create new company" or "Login to another account"

## **Step 4: Verify Integration**

- If you have successfully entered the bitCRM dashboard, your Shopify is already integrated with bitCRM.
