## Why use AI Studio

AI Studio allows you to build agents that handle inquiries, provide recommendations, and automate sales tasks. It is distinct from other tools in the bitbybit ecosystem.

**Difference between bitAI and AI Studio:**

| Feature              | bitAI                                   | AI Studio                            |
| :------------------- | :-------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------- |
| **Primary Goal**     | Backend Management                      | Frontend Engagement                  |
| **Function**         | Sorts products and manages collections. | Talks to customers and closes sales. |
| **User Interaction** | Internal tool for merchants.            | External chatbot for customers.      |

## Configuration steps

Follow these steps to create and launch your AI agent.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new agent">
    1. Go to the [**AI Studio dashboard**](https://app.bitbybit.studio/ai-studio/ai-agent).
    2. Click **Create new agent**.

    ![Screenshot: The AI Studio dashboard highlighting the Create new agent button](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67cac0b64964400d32aba97c_AD_4nXdPEz6AekLit7H2yqUQQuZlIgcJTMx4TauxtTCoVSH4zouWIEc3VouQL7iUClftD7t-xHNaPNcKJq7-wxkowjF2Qx2dTo3E2-pW2YsDAniHvHm7oQPJC8eFjq9AC5wbjHZ7TJrW.png)

    <Tip>
      You can create multiple agents if you want to test different strategies or personalities.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the model and identity">
    Select the "Brain" of your agent and define who it is.

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Model Selection">
        Choose the AI model type that powers your agent.

        - **Conversation:** Powered by **Gemini 2.5 Flash**. Best for natural, fluid dialogue.
        - **Reasoning:** Optimized for complex logic and problem-solving. (**GPT: 4.1 Mini**)

        ![Screenshot: Model selection showing Conversation and Reasoning options](/images/Screenshot2025-12-19135459.png)
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Agent Details">
        Fill in the identity fields:

        - **Chatbot Name:** The internal name for your reference.
        - **Role:** The job title (e.g., "Sales Assistant").
        - **Brand Name:** The name customers will see.
        - **Description:** **Critical.** Provide detailed context about your business. The more detail you provide here, the smarter your AI will be.

        ![Screenshot: The agent details form including name and description](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67cac0b73a1c4e9ed47140c9_AD_4nXeM2r4RnHwLybOpGxX5qPk5PANTRsLSzb20CvtUbH3VKhiT0VXNZhW9QqMW0_zSq_C7BXO0CF-X6nP9EEWkS4gJ3qDg33FCK6-KgvSEAy82Djs_FSojLsUFTHT6si5QtWgrKHGE.png)
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the Skillset">
    Skills define what actions your AI can perform. Scroll down to the **Skillset** section, toggle on the skills you need, and click them to configure settings.

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Product Recommendation">
        Allows the AI to suggest items from your catalog based on customer preferences.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Order Tracking">
        Enables the AI to check shipping status and provide real-time updates.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Create Order">
        **High Value.** Automatically generates orders and checkout links based on the conversation.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="FAQ & Data Collection">
        - **FAQ:** Answers customer inquiries using files you upload.
        - **Data Collection:** Gathers specific data (like email or location) for retargeting.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test your agent">
    Before going live, use the **AI Playground** on the right side of the screen.

    1. Type a message as if you were a customer (e.g., "Where is my order?" or "Recommend a shoe").
    2. Verify that the AI responds according to the identity and skills you set.

    ![Screenshot: The AI Playground showing a test conversation](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67cac0b600d68cdaa2633a82_AD_4nXe1DK2B66KrH8nWshP73ZICYcc8x9FNYEavPV1vf8cvqHiVlm4bxjrnZ_Oan3VVz4LYH16C2ifsl93SMqL_DoMH8oRWjUoCz7arC4twkw5X8c5c18k4CAYr_iC96eDqrikJzLyeQQ.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
