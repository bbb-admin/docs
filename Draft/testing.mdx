Assigning the correct roles ensures your team has access to the tools they need while keeping sensitive company settings secure.

<Info>
  **Prerequisite:** Only **Super Admins** and **Admins** can invite new users or modify the roles of existing team members.
</Info>

## Role definitions

### Super Admin
The **Super Admin** has ultimate authority over the workspace.
* **Best for:** Business owners.
* **Key Capability:** This is the *only* role that can delete the company workspace.
* **Access:** Full access to all features, billing, and settings.

### Admin
The **Admin** manages day-to-day operations and team structure.
* **Best for:** Managers and Team Leads.
* **Key Capability:** Can manage users, billing, and settings.
* **Limitation:** Cannot delete the company workspace.

### Agent
The **Agent** role is strictly for customer support handling.
* **Best for:** Customer Service Representatives (CSRs).
* **Access:** Focuses on Tickets and Live Chat.
* **Limitation:** No access to internal apps (bitCRM, bitApp) or Analytics.

### Regular User
The **Regular User** role is for internal employees who do not do support work.
* **Best for:** General staff and stakeholders.
* **Access:** Can use internal apps (bitCRM, bitApp) and view Analytics.
* **Limitation:** Cannot manage tickets or chat with customers.

## How to manage permissions

If a team member needs different access (e.g., a Regular User needs to help with support tickets), you can update their authority settings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access User & Authority">
    Go to **Settings** and click on **User & Authority**.
     ![Screenshot2025 09 11131958 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-09-11131958.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a user">
    Click **Add User** to invite someone new, or click the **Edit** icon next to an existing user.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define the role">
    Enter the user's email (if new) and select their primary role (e.g., **Regular User**) from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant specific access">
    If you need a Regular User to handle tickets without making them a full Admin, check the box labeled **Agent Access**.
     ![Screenshot2025 09 11132126 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-09-11132126.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invite or save">
    Click **Send Invite** (for new users) or **Save** (for existing users) to apply the changes immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>
