AI Studio allows you to build custom AI Agents that handle inquiries, recommend products, and automate workflows. Unlike standard automation, these agents use advanced language models to provide natural, human-like responses.

For a visual walkthrough, watch the video below.

<Info>
  **Prerequisite:** To use advanced models like GPT-o3 Mini, you must be on the **Standard Plan** or higher.
</Info>

## AI Studio vs. bitAI

It is important to understand the difference between our two AI products so you know which one to use.

| Feature | AI Studio | bitAI |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Goal** | Customer Engagement | Inventory Management |
| **Function** | Chatbots & Support Automation | Product Sorting & Collections |
| **Interaction** | Talks to customers directly | Works in the background |

## Setup process

Follow these steps to create your first AI chatbot.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new agent">
    Navigate to the [AI Studio dashboard](https://app.bitbybit.studio/ai-studio/ai-agent) and click **Create new agent**.
    ![Screenshot: The AI Studio dashboard showing the Create New Agent button](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67cac0b64964400d32aba97c_AD_4nXdPEz6AekLit7H2yqUQQuZlIgcJTMx4TauxtTCoVSH4zouWIEc3VouQL7iUClftD7t-xHNaPNcKJq7-wxkowjF2Qx2dTo3E2-pW2YsDAniHvHm7oQPJC8eFjq9AC5wbjHZ7TJrW.png)
    <Tip>You can create multiple agents to test different personalities or strategies.</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your AI model">
    Choose the AI model that powers your agent.
    ![Screenshot: The model selection screen](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67d90d9e2575606526457121_AD_4nXfatmPH6fStavM2v00OfZz_ttrC92BAxkxWDH4Z5vavto-ZmYoLuDrUsXzhmkm7VOggYq_SaIIeWYlF-WUQ-G_FEsgAPbQ2CZe0gTRFUCGrkvTTOL7tc-x1Vk4lyVGI-sMslOiYlg.png)
    <Note>**GPT-o3 Mini** requires a Standard Plan upgrade.</Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure agent details">
    Fill in the **Chatbot Name**, **Role**, **Brand Name**, and **Description**.
    ![Screenshot: The chatbot details configuration form](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67cac0b73a1c4e9ed47140c9_AD_4nXeM2r4RnHwLybOpGxX5qPk5PANTRsLSzb20CvtUbH3VKhiT0VXNZhW9QqMW0_zSq_C7BXO0CF-X6nP9EEWkS4gJ3qDg33FCK6-KgvSEAy82Djs_FSojLsUFTHT6si5QtWgrKHGE.png)
    <Tip>**Pro Tip:** Be as detailed as possible in the **Description** field. The more context you provide about your business, the better the AI will understand how to answer.</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable skillsets">
    Scroll down to the **Skillset** section. Click on a skill to toggle it on and configure its settings:
    * **Product Recommendation:** Suggests products based on browsing history.
    * **Order Tracking:** Provides real-time shipping updates.
    * **FAQ:** Answers common questions using your uploaded files.
    * **Create Order:** Generates draft orders for customers in chat.
    * **Data Collection:** Gathers emails or phone numbers for marketing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test your chatbot">
    Once configured, use the **AI Playground** on the right side of the screen to start a conversation. This allows you to verify that the AI responds correctly before you go live.
    ![Screenshot: The AI Playground testing interface](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67cac0b600d68cdaa2633a82_AD_4nXe1DK2B66KrH8nWshP73ZICYcc8x9FNYEavPV1vf8cvqHiVlm4bxjrnZ_Oan3VVz4LYH16C2ifsl93SMqL_DoMH8oRWjUoCz7arC4twkw5X8c5c18k4CAYr_iC96eDqrikJzLyeQQ.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
