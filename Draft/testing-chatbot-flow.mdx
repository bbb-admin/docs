## Why create a chatbot flow

Chatbot flows allow you to automate responses to common queries, categorize tickets automatically, and guide customers through a menu system without human intervention.

**Channel Capabilities:**

The available features depend on which channel you are using.

## Configuration steps

Follow these steps to build your automation flow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new chatbot">
    1. Go to the **bitChat dashboard** and click **Chatbot** in the sidebar.
    2. Click the **Create new chatbot +** button in the top right.

    ![Screenshot: The chatbot dashboard highlighting the Create new chatbot button](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/669a2d7295996d732efcd1f9_AD_4nXeYdJYahJ-qXaxbu_zdqq6eRRHkb1zv6q5VSojqDuUnZP-4eIQCQ1ukcC7Q8KmakzhTTv6PWOnD7cIjXZsxwQhSWbibD44cvs-WLRFsfI3IcB3p2UayC3rSKPZvVvTCT68cpaSf_8hZ45hIhnytb3Dzxf8.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the channel">
    Choose the channel for this specific flow. The settings will adjust based on your selection.

    ![Screenshot: The channel selection screen showing WhatsApp and Instagram options](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/669a2d7267c10f74f139a768_AD_4nXcBTDwgF9qq2jZuaIUDFm1q0RMWUvltvGMvyz4xWnmcl7Q-yQ29FU5tu0tydMLaMq2eD8BnCO6UEzRFKQMMfrCwOTGCow755Q5mR4lgcd4gOVK0X_rdm6ayyTGCesth5NhydgjgKHdUNhUEU5Pl16nLTIw.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the Trigger">
    The "Trigger" is the first message that starts the bot. Configure the conditions below.

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Keyword Settings">
        - **Fallback Message:** Choose what happens if the user replies with something the bot doesn't understand later in the flow.
        - **Keyword Condition:**
          - **Is contain:** Triggers if the word is anywhere in the sentence (e.g., "Hello" triggers on "Hello there").
          - **Is exact:** Triggers only on an exact match.
        - **Input Keyword:** Type your keywords and press Enter.

        ![Screenshot: The keyword condition settings](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/669a38bd6389266d151b3603_AD_4nXdEiSF7joBa5N06CRsvf76tBNDhg74qxcAgeTZf1x0y6Yn1_RVDisd4_7O18UF7BSUPFB41qBfEOI0aRac1agG98yf2QGAugcVbKp50En2Set7hqWrSVwOpPHArJ0gLMVyLmDWljeM1qw_8LrwJht-yO5ZS.png)

        ![Screenshot: The keyword input field](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/669a391b69ba41945e7cd542_AD_4nXcgzLLIqq5Pbdjw8Rg291GnXWGAOwmUZ7wIVlKA-4T0OR1xzrh_kgmt_fYyhX0ZR-OTvFM2xqjreK466P37qHwnSpo3thd4b6Xtxl94O8-pRlkWYzH2kS10DiYWUFERe3yuS_9inwHnfQEwASD6QGhbaNk.png)
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Categorization & Media">
        - **Ticket Category/Tag:** Automatically tag customers who trigger this flow (e.g., "Potential Lead").
        - **Media:** Drag and drop an image to send a visual response along with your text.

        ![Screenshot: The settings for ticket categorization and media upload](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/669a2d7269c590bec3297015_AD_4nXc_mZQGLrg9qsZQwER6znRElNs8mCKA0xNvEKl2laOLxZavuFZ8Uo2UFZ-nE13CDttr2lNrgh3wM2UE4HGAJPAHXsVlPckwawHp3GY60X3dXMjaff-wvQeCotiNAOzuJy0g0TAlB5EtDBeIZj9tomkhBVgK.png)
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the Reply Options">
    Define the message the user receives and the options they can click. This step varies by channel.

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="For WhatsApp App & Instagram">
        1. **Message:** Type your response text.
        2. **Reply Options:** List the text options users can reply with.

        ![Screenshot: Reply configuration for WhatsApp App](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/669a39c774131814a39da805_AD_4nXevrkq_X9KPpfsShg0UOVtgoq2SkPJ6T2WYrQQirrmJbSlwuEnDlNdysI0Z5TGMuPik107rLpy-RAGXaReIFfM4jn-diyDyWxWyKti1CLHR9bZpBtsK7M-Vcn3iBr9ms4eJciAmrOuGZDWqQ5ElgHAKiJSJ.png)
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="For WhatsApp Cloud API">
        1. **Message:** Type your response text.
        2. **Reply Options:** Choose a format.
           - **Buttons:** Create up to 3 clickable buttons.
           - **Options List:** Create a menu with up to 10 items.

        ![Screenshot: Comparison between Buttons and Options List in Cloud API](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/669a33fa5ed0c265c7df39cd_AD_4nXcBULWHifOQ5ItLdfUIeROSt87eu2uKaOWwaU1JFxZR4fn34Z9WxXehMZT8yiHfR-XvyEFqkM9ioDoWDrvQ-sQTsjq0EWjCQZvLeiy-iVMPaooFZ2osIzUn8FsYQlXRf15VQg2NhQYV0IDHcguTo33CEGuh.png)
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
    Click **Save** to enter the flow editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build the Next Flow">
    You will now see the Flow Board.

    1. Click on one of the **Reply Options** you just created.
    2. Choose an outcome:
       - **Action:** Perform a backend task (e.g., Add Tag).
       - **Message:** Send another message (Continue the conversation).
       - **Back (App/IG only):** Return to the previous menu.

    ![Screenshot: Creating the next flow step on the board](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/669a2d72ffd0c6497eec0658_AD_4nXd8wviwyipQwP8U0aSnHA6lT15ki1bzrK_zwriqhDXBJiOaJ55dw0ggUCdecj15sFiaG_cJ6iFzyfZFrPboeIPT8OoUj9UbkqTr_tZjjn_vjzMsZgg2yTimFe1P1f4LdPi_uk9cXWQ_JtV1bYSk8AIocvgV.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Activate">
    1. Click the **Save** icon in the top right.
    2. Toggle the switch next to the title to **Active**.

    ![Screenshot: The toggle switch to activate the chatbot flow](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/669a2d724b732665a4925e1d_AD_4nXdmHfzv7HD9XQtLrBT4hJJ_Qcmn-MOhIS1r698_xIxYuakKpYdnqMLUiM2tzLB4vNWUb3CKF3MPWbwJeZ7kmR4Ov_X5VDPaPzLN-a_1iSmE55O-m69ZLLZprCCPFDgn2JAAM_f3ps8AcvH2ENldBHrzGkg.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
