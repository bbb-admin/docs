---
title: "How to Edit or Delete a Circle Image Grid"
---

Quickly update or remove your Circle Image Grid to keep your app layout fresh and relevant. This feature provides flexibility in design, making adjustments straightforward. Here’s a simple guide to managing it effectively.

## **Step 1:** Access your [bitApp dashboard](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitapp/design)

## **Step 2: Open the Circle Image Grid**

* Click “Circle Image Grid” in your mobile mockup, then it will appear in the sidebar.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66d9675800d6e3c06861d644_AD_4nXflFOAfDIDP0gpR0QEmDiJZ8j5X3EPOXLXrxAxj3M5Ry4_BmGEoz0Ek7MahbQ5WCVy3QkX7Y2yQ-2sN32GhoCzs56CheYQ5CMlC2Hgr5yEm88LrXOllCosX99e19C6whbZXQsAIboVZpaqU7gPIApCHOfw.png)Click “Circle Image Grid”‍

## **Step 3: Delete Circle Image Grid section**

* Click the "Trash can" icon and click “Delete”.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66de99639d29f9c2bceecc2e_AD_4nXcX4YrX1y75OymHEWUOMgVoI0qx1oH_MM-bNZ1gu4OZBCKApT3_sVYyb3kRVw9bi9XixgaPK4yVmS4za4utW4li4BPoF_cmR3pnEEqfguhE1S30B1hzc6kiSiSs3Wyt0K7z-uIInxuijNoWdPpOq0wrtuj4.png)Click the "Trash can"![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66de9963cb0b09a66c33dc9c_AD_4nXd37W-DJJbnoB4nhbywBdLZSrcXFtmLDO7SlwMwR3btuZtqUVipyvqDCjeQIxSo0elZ7RrAROne-OOs3dYLemIrW29dmdYnE3Bew_2gNDPXdZZOImPai1Ji8xbqyFBBTs75uFjegu0G1fuAGSqtu-i5qA07.png)Click “Delete”‍
