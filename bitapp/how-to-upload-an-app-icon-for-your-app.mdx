---
title: "How to Upload an App Icon for Your App"
---

Add a custom icon to your bitApp to strengthen brand recognition. This tutorial details how to upload and preview your app icon for a polished, branded look on all platforms.

## **Step 1: Access Your BitApp Dashboard**

- Go to your [bitApp dashboard](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitapp/design).

## **Step 2: Navigate to Branding**

- Click on "Branding".

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66f231eff265ded09dbabe15_AD_4nXf1VzB8vo2G4rkhbVpeJdZ-9yc9cfJtaHN8iN6tp6CWywIFRIxqxL4eGw-kAPZ6614gkwBh8wGuMtr7XI-3st7BTM9zH-Vwje1oLT_FoWMrWgDH2daSUdpQUODDz4AGuOW2760ZLm5G606U2xPeFij71eZb.png)

Click "Branding"

## **Step 3: Upload App Icon**

- Click "Upload Image" and select your app icon file with an image size 1024 x 1024 pixels.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66f231f0ebee3dcd92e3ca3e_AD_4nXeVbKpDZbagKcGvl8RkkWKptKvcLdVGlE27b48JHXW1lcFyGb1KO1lbWNIKJrH-9xrncLednUxNyAcM452gbUYRrOHNqSQZ7Xhggc1vtVEmrlBhCXyYkNifYeRx3BQnKCpMZLb9Nu-6F_V6P2Uk44SWJXae.png)

Click "Upload Image"

## **Step 4: Save Changes & Preview**

- Click "Save changes" and click “App Icon menu” to preview your page.

Click "Save changes"
