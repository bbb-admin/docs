---
title: "Understanding the Unsubscribe Option for Messages"
---

Enabling the unsubscribe option in bitChat is essential for managing customer preferences and ensuring compliance with messaging standards. This feature allows users to opt-out easily, enhancing customer experience and maintaining trust. Follow these steps to activate the unsubscribe settings.

## **Step 1:** Go to [bitChat Settings chatbot](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitchat/settings?section=chatbot) dashboard.

## **Step 2:** Scroll down to find "Unsubscribe" then click "toggle" to green.
