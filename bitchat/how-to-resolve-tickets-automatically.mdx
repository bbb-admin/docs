---
title: "How to Resolve Tickets Automatically"
---

Automating ticket resolution in bitChat saves time by closing inactive or resolved tickets without manual intervention. This guide explains how to enable and configure auto-resolve settings to streamline your support workflow.

## **Step 1:** Go to [bitChat Settings - Live Chat & Ticket](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitchat/settings?section=livechat-ticket) dashboard.

## **Step 2:** Click toggle to green.
