**Step 1: Access the Order Dashboard**

Navigate to the **Commerce** or **Orders** section in your bitbybit dashboard to view your current order list.

**Step 2: Export Orders**

1. Locate the **Export** button (usually found at the top of the order list).
2. **Click Export** to initiate the download process.
3. Your order data will be downloaded to your device in **CSV** or **Excel** format.

**Congratulations! You have successfully exported your commerce order data.**
