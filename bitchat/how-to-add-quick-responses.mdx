---
title: "How to Add Quick Responses"
---

Quick responses in bitChat allow you to respond instantly to customer inquiries, providing pre-set answers to frequently asked questions. This feature helps streamline communication and improve customer satisfaction. Learn how to configure and add quick responses to your chat widget.

## **Step 1:** Access the bitChat Chat widget Dashboard

* Go to the [bitChat Chat widget](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitchat/wa-widget) dashboard and enable toggle “Quick responses”  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66ff500711dd23cdd5b67c06_AD_4nXe5FATpkPLNVjTMqguKPbNhQ5aIlEV76FBkbu9VudC9avHqiL-cgKOskXYMJbgUgFQzmTP2vA3yH7kPLQ6hhoL4MSxdj0hMhFXcKo2j9OKDVnxbypPcoQrcA4fsF2j_mjcOJSKH8OLf9-ND--jIjL3-Y5Q.png)Enable toggle “Quick responses”‍

## **Step 2:** Add Quick Response

* Enter the Button text and select chatbot on dropdown. You can add more responses by clicking the “+” icon.

You can add more by clicking the “+”
