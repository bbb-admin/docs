---
title: "How to Start Chat with a New Number"
---

Start customer chats instantly using a new phone number. This guide details the steps for starting a chat with a new number, whether using pre-made templates or custom message templates.

## **Step 1:** Go to [bitChat Livechat](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitchat/livechat) dashboard and click "Source channel menu".

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66fe64ae7b51859ac3bd9c49_AD_4nXdYT39MBFw5Kta3NPJ9k4lvmC40Z292i1I75NTkhk-necajJNJEocqqXuf6T9FDHzsdu-zRJpivPD40zA55wdHQiITrclBKVPLFamyPp6Sbs2TXzT-fGWfWy3_9dKN8XPUY2k1DUfNtSUZhsJr-bk6wnPOz.png)Click "Source channel menu"‍

## **Step 2:** Choose a source from the dropdown.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66fe64aea668c61d30f1eedb_AD_4nXddsCNZ5USz5PhZiywrCF8EZbuUPgmscCy0LS1rLGFAxVuv5Xz_A0K9bSgHMfp1SWpSDsC9b-UWwEETjSjTUSpd6DEKjp1VTtxH4xEgpt9pUyTDnxXQMJ438nGgNubnt29GZHhYVa1KTDxhcG2lqhT-KTao.png)Choose a source‍

### **For WhatsApp Cloud API:**

* Click "WhatsApp Cloud API".
* Choose the "Message template" or "Create new template" click How to Create A Chat Initiation Template to see the guide.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66fe64ae2df5fb1bd6e8aa8c_AD_4nXfFYOp0HJ_eAEwHCcreWtVQ02DjJbc8k98pJOH5dluKwI65laYQYoxhdTXOvELNdaKIVjHLZSbo72xK1wGH8j5WNOznMYmiBSbHSB8PWjzVex_i7eXKtZcQBJiaGEHbbxMFESx186J0vK-j0v0Lmbnc0byG.png)Choose the "Message template" or "Create new template"* Enter "Phone number" and click "Start chat"  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66fe64ae9f98b784fa3179e7_AD_4nXeEkxBZPm8HfrpwQOuK8pP9F8TX8W-15AQ43iM4VCCh_eM9anbBqNE1jwPxiqH4uuclBk7sfwfsSytHShJNrArxTwRbda7veTLUbBxHPhD9OuJ-RCcQXtE5kQntqCBjMgtXGdfmbzxyrelM8lj2Bgwns5Q.png)Click "Start chat"‍

### **For WhatsApp Business App:**

* Click "WhatsApp Business App".
*  Enter "Phone number" and click "Start chat"
