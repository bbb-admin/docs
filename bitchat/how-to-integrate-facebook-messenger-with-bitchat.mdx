---
title: "How to Integrate Facebook Messenger with bitChat"
description: "Integrating Facebook Messenger with bitChat allows you to streamline customer communication by managing messages from your Facebook Page directly within the bitChat platform. Follow these steps to connect your Facebook Messenger to bitChat:"
---

import {Step, StepList, Alert} from "@mintlify/components";

## Step 1: Access the Integrations Page

1. Navigate to the [Integrations page](https://dev.bitbybit.studio/integrations).
2. In the Communication Channel section, locate **Facebook** and click the **Connect** button.

## Step 2: Connect with Facebook

1. Click on **Connect with Facebook**.
2. You will be redirected to a Meta login page. Enter your Facebook credentials and log in.
3. If prompted, grant the necessary permissions to allow bitChat to access your Facebook account.

## Step 3: Select Your Business

1. On the "Choose the Businesses you want bitChat to access" page, select **Opt in to all current and future Businesses**.
2. Click **Continue**.

## Step 4: Select Your Facebook Page

1. On the "Choose the Pages you want bitChat to access" page, select **Opt in to all current and future Pages**.
2. Alternatively, you can select **Opt in to current Pages only** and choose specific pages.
3. Click **Continue**.

## Step 5: Review Permissions

1. Review all permission requests.
2. Once everything is correct, click the **Got it** button.

## Step 6: Confirm Connection

1. You will be redirected back to the integrations page.
2. Select the Facebook Page you want to connect.
3. Click **Continue**.
4. Wait a few moments until the notification **"Successfully connected to Facebook Page"** appears.

## Step 7: Access Live Chat

Your Facebook Messenger is now successfully connected to bitChat. You can view and manage customer conversations by navigating to the [Live Chat](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitchat/livechat) section.
