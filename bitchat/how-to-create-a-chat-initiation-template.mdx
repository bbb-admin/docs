---
title: "How to Create a Chat Initiation Template"
---

Setting up a chat initiation template in bitChat ensures that your customers receive a welcoming and consistent message when they connect through WhatsApp. This feature is crucial for guiding conversations and establishing a professional tone right from the start. Follow these steps to create a custom template that fits your communication style.

## Step 1: Go to [bitChat Settings - WhatsApp Cloud API](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitchat/settings?section=whatsapp-cloud-api) dashboard

## Step 2: Scroll to "Chat initiation template" and click "Create new template"

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/6705cd979fbcf5b8fab7547b_AD_4nXf_F5__i9QSFrOnKbWKNpBamed-1sNtajRZ5ROCXmwdTUsCVyL4puMf_xCiy1uWJtuyEjDZKNs4SJUisFHWSOKQ1--PX4E9F3tYY-moreMzgB2PR4X5IW5mA3CxKAtzSh56bV92EcNZwWOsLYTZ9yv3aj8.png)Click "Create new template"‍

## Step 3: Fill in template name, choose the language, and message

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/6706daee96d83352d842c723_AD_4nXfSHsJFtVd5oB8yzs4pPxIQCTv8C1lqwiMCKkp9QtNHUWB1PPs_u9fAEXrwECw9ecmCooNeN_8mrTV2SP3NdwjKb_l0o7la63QQ8y9EN85T3TX9RehjoVmZROiYD_4Px-WF9CDRmWiPb2rwXDz1c6HtL126.png)Fill in template name, choose the language, and message‍

## Step 4: Click "Save template"

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/6705cd970ce27de11bbbd2ae_AD_4nXd5mCOJ1LBYClp1xba6HGHp8f-Tj0W6o3uXk8VuyKnh2tJj8WALukfU_YXCgiiE7q5a9QWXV-hOszzlpER9w_kP2Oy1IE-yKiamqCi7MqwIfF3--BdE-EXdCr5rZ4WXcmPJKaCS18qBYUl9AyqKNUfRwrc.png)Click "Save template"Once your message template is created test your chat initiation to ensure it successfully or see [How to Start Chat with a New Number](https://www.bitbybit.studio/knowledge-base/how-to-start-chat-with-a-new-number) to see further.
