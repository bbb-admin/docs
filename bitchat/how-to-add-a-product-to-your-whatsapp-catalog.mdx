---
title: "How to Add a Product to Your WhatsApp Catalog"
---

Add products to your WhatsApp Catalog by importing from Shopify or creating them manually. Products will be reviewed by Meta before appearing in the catalog.

## **Step 1: Access the Product Dashboard**

* Log in to your [Product dashboard](https://app.bitbybit.studio/products).

## **Step 2: You Can Choose an Option**

* **Option 1:** Click **"Import from Shopify"**.

Enter the key product details in each section to complete your listing:

* **Product Detail:** title, description, image.
* **Inventory** *(optional)*: fill out SKU & brand details for your products*.*
* **Display on WhatsApp Catalog:** to show your product to WhatsApp catalog, toggle this on.
* **Pricing:** enter price and currency.
* **Product Type & Tags** *(optional)*: for organizing your catalog.
