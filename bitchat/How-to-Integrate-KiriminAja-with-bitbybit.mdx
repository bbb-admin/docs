## **Pre-requisite: Create Company Account & Find Company ID**

1. Create your company account at [**<u>https://bitbybit.studio/</u>**](https://bitbybit.studio/)
2. After logging in, go to **Settings**
3. Find your **Company ID**

**Need help finding your Company ID?**\
Follow this official guide:\
[<u>https://knowledge.bitbybit.studio/settings/how-to-get-company-ID</u>](https://knowledge.bitbybit.studio/settings/how-to-get-company-ID)

You will need this Company ID during the KiriminAja integration request.

## **Step 4: Submit Integration Request for bitbybit**

<img
  src="/images/0d827e26-0d82-4c2d-9e78-121240dbf753(4).jpg"
  alt="0d827e26 0d82 4c2d 9e78 121240dbf753(4)"
  title="0d827e26 0d82 4c2d 9e78 121240dbf753(4)"
  style={{ width:"48%" }}
/>

- Select **Request Integrasi**
- Choose **bitbybit** as the integration partner
- Input your **Company ID from bitbybit**
- Submit the request

---

## **Step 5: Wait for KiriminAja Approval**

- KiriminAja will review your request
- Approval is typically completed **as fast as 2 hours**

---

## **Step 6: Copy Setup Key from KiriminAja**

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2026-01-14163214.png"
  alt="Screenshot2026 01 14163214"
  title="Screenshot2026 01 14163214"
  style={{ width:"50%" }}
/>

- Once approved, open the **integration detail/action page**
- Copy the **Setup Key** provided by KiriminAja

---

## **Step 7: Open bitbybit Platform**

- Log in to your **bitbybit platform**

---

## **Step 8: Connect KiriminAja on bitbybit**

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2026-01-14163313.png"

/>

- Go to the **Integrations** menu
- Click **Connect to KiriminAja**

---

## **Step 9: Paste Setup Key**

<img
  src="/images/image(25).png"
  alt="Image(25)"
  title="Image(25)"
  style={{ width:"45%" }}
/>

- Paste the **Setup Key** copied from KiriminAja into the integration request field on bitbybit

---

## **Step 10: Save and Complete the Connection**

- Click **Save**
- Once saved successfully, your KiriminAja account is now connected to bitbybit
