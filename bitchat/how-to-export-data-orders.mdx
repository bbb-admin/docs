Exporting your order data allows you to store reports or analyze performance externally.

## **Step 1: Access the** [**Order Dashboard**](https://app.bitbybit.studio/commerce/orders)

## **Step 2: Export Orders**

* Click **Export** to download your order data in CSV or Excel format.
