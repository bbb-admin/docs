title: "How to Create a WhatsApp Form"
---

Building a WhatsApp Form within bitChat allows you to gather information efficiently. With customizable fields you can design forms that suit your needs. This tutorial will guide you step-by-step to create and optimize your form for better data collection.

## Step 1: Go to [bitChat WhatsApp Form](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitchat/whatsapp-form) dashboard

## Step 2: Click "Create new form" and fill in “Form name” the click “Create form”

## Step 3: Click "Add section"

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66fe6d4473b99764cd102e72_AD_4nXdA3erKP3LQLtW-oTx0rTIVitpl2HO63azaa5efJxMulirPshrCmJKIWtLdGmds-Noq1mHTewTAJOeJjlRTF535FGzOFdp_Py_YmSFDC45lzpQIz9A9RIIpTWSrCmuDdknXdUY2XJDFU8SiRxaZMwv8Xe9e.png)Click "Add section"  
1. **Text**: fill in headings, caption, and body text, then save.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/6705cc57e3f1d08648cb532f_AD_4nXdROm35anVmFjRdXWKlfx2W0KTZSrxhID-06xhlM8ejW9koS-5j9rOw_RQrQkx43Uj7WaAeO5ackWF4dGJ8wPeoN7I-9r-Aa3xNoAIgkTC1DmJkCK014q_AQvtL_cjTgXRfUETQ8sO9fFn0oT8FNWYLoQb1.png)Fill in necessary fields‍

2. **Media**: Upload an image and save.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/6705cc57100cd24ed3d9d918_AD_4nXcXM8oGke9a3UJIdCijqVQ5apWmLx5NMIl_VnUFCokfzH_baCWKJ9zQnzSzk7YYztbD-GzKD27knOk7rtvpFr3eIsouHmUne8J0gLDB8gglyD4LPs6_xYfh6nNn2XbOwKhak-nh6QFGy9PZx3CukIykX2Op.png)Click "Upload Image" and "Save"‍

3. **Text answer**: Add short/paragraph answers or datetime input. Sort fields by marking them “Required” and save.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/6705cc5739f54ed03d6c5228_AD_4nXeVkKY2sY-Sy4CYpDfeW59dkF0T4mW5I9ZVIY9-nJbeCTUbJSzbyS_nwCLEU1AxEoaaVJWoq9aFZ_YlrpqsGdtiVYwqoER5AyxV6T9tjCXh_U0H2ee5N9_iy1OuGqYvDvD2s2APlEfsrV5nkFzEKnODF4Q1.png)Add "Text answer"‍

4. **Selection**: Create dropdowns, multiple, or single-choice fields. Sort fields by marking them “Required” and save changes.
