---
title: "How to Edit Your Product Variants"
---

Update product variant details of an existing product in your catalog.

## **Step 1: Access the Product Dashboard**

* Log in to your [Product dashboard](https://app.bitbybit.studio/products).

## **Step 2: Select the Product**

* Choose and click on the product you want to edit.

* **Scroll down to the** **Variants** **s**ection, then click **Edit** on the desired variant.

* After editing, click Save Changes to update the variant.
