title: "How to Edit or Delete a Quick Reply Template"
---

Managing quick reply templates in bitChat makes customer interactions more efficient. This guide explains how to edit or delete quick reply templates, allowing you to keep your response options up-to-date and relevant.

## **Step 1:** Go to [bitChat Quick reply template](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitchat/quickreply) dashboard.

## **Step 2:** Click on the desired template to make edits.
