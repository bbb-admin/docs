---
title: "How to Partially Fulfill a Commerce Item"
---

Handle cases where only some items in an order can be fulfilled, such as limited stock or split shipments.

## **Step 1: Access the** [**Order Dashboard**](https://app.bitbybit.studio/commerce/orders)

## **Step 2: Select a Partially Fulfilled Order**

* Choose the order marked with **"Partially Fulfilled"**.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/687243bb88eb67bdb2632db7_AD_4nXcvE_UDhul6eczb9xOJN-4cbC8-g7c_ABh9k07EF74spPQwPtKlG0SDfZ-yWOPcPXtxFI8JgbyjoFUDE7xKmp-nyNDtGaUx98CR4-deXIIZCC4eBhlObjK9YcnpFEv_cs6TyeS92g.png)Choose the order marked with "Partially Fulfilled"## **Step 3: Complete Remaining Fulfillment**

* Scroll down, click **Fulfill Item**.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/687242ba55180fc6055a6b79_AD_4nXejwHYSxBNrLsCiqrX6M4wY0kIcZZ2-2kHCeZwABmXCD8tX488w6j4G6ixQFpFT-OcfLNK3racpxCjd3Ab60ScwPjIe5giKPytQDixLWPrukgl8iAbsV74nwYEo1bctoIFGh1X6.png)Click Fulfill Item* Fill in the necessary fields and click **Fulfill**.
