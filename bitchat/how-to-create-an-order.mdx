---
title: "How to Create a Commerce Order"
---

Manually create a customer order by adding products, payment details, shipping info, and selecting the customer.

## **Step 1: Access the** [**Order Dashboard**](https://app.bitbybit.studio/commerce/orders)

* Click **Add New Order**.

## **Step 2: Add Items**

* Click **Add Items** and select products, then input the quantity for each.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/68724160d09e315dde8ee57f_AD_4nXe1urJBiUXY8SDT466EQFC5NqPgQ0aNZZxJP0H_ImK8YmftU1mBxjKcWc5nmDxrHRRShAjXVIIkDgi6qw22nevqQyfcDqJ5q0EC7ArGsOlunhVSdhuwVxqUpi2IRrKbzvOD9IJl.png)Click Add Items and select products## **Step 3: Select Customer**

* Select **Customer**, If the customer already has an address saved, it will be auto-filled.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/68724160d09e315dde8ee585_AD_4nXcafxRLmGtLKZtpteffY1OqBrdm09SSsTbgtVg2X8AICMD7e_DJcBmcHn8EPO72FuH-RlJbpPNd6w8eMr_41awD0ZF7GgZuTexDNnLTx5NxbCeFXughkAdbuqN3UkHTotlA3GTz2A.png)Select Customer## **Step 4: Add Address**

* If a customer does not have an address yet, click **Add Address** and fill in the details.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/68724160d09e315dde8ee588_AD_4nXf4FLHR-fGg3Y3RCTJK33XY8XHgpQr0DgpaRi1-SNehqQA8JVwuUX3LDDUkvNtwV2rEwT5Ovr8l5KR5TshTVUJ8wtLpfanfXy9pdyS1z_s0BmJFII2vtb-pKT8pirq5kkr-2IuzNA.png)Click Add Address and fill in the details## **Step 5: Add Shipping**

* Click **Add Shipping**, select a shipping method, complete the form, and click **Add Shipping**.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/68724160d09e315dde8ee58b_AD_4nXfEnOJ_m99gJJ4hrcJL-tc0YW180avFuR6a7RduZ0bQXgWGYqxXX0h4aDD-4mBETOFEqn4VhLGB-nYaXRza2M0kfO5dhg-0cz_CcnHh1lpuFlhjWZlI1mMhk0dviX3QpTg_rjtpcw.png)Click Add Shipping## **Step 6: Add Payment Method**

* **Custom Payment**: Click **Custom Payment**, choose a saved method or create a new one by filling the available fields. Then click **Add Payment Method**.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/68724160d09e315dde8ee58e_AD_4nXemQqh1HrPDzncycnIUspy7RduDVZaVsRCyndUiYYpuRMoF37XLgMin71OQf-BfXbZ-GiejKpLLh8Ttx10PjGJAmvUOJ-eAIvIvGhvXbIYwi0Vtd-miVqF9xSa0Ps64dteGWOPI.png)Custom Payment* **Xendit Payment**: Go to the **Integration Dashboard** or read [How to Enable Xendit Payment Link on bitbybit](https://www.bitbybit.studio/knowledge-base/how-to-enable-xendit-payment-link-on-bitbybit) to follow the setup guide.

## **Step 7: Add Notes and Tags (Optional)**

* Add **notes from customers** (if available).
* Add **order tags** to categorize the order (optional).  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/68724160d09e315dde8ee582_AD_4nXfWz9UH6F3mczo42nayu3-sjw_4_xdSkKcubRkiVlB-2i5Wzlg1RndkaaDTy0KswcbQL0KSXjspO-YB-eZlfkkojJ3M2jaeIAUFi8wv9wP07JgK2mLd8etCENUg2mE3Y3L1QWimEg.png)Add notes from customers and order tags## **Step 8: Create Order**

Click **Create Order** to complete the process.
