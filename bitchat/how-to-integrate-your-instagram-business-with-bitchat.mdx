# How to Integrate Your Instagram Business with bitChat

Integrating your Instagram Business account with bitChat enables seamless communication with your customers, enhancing engagement and support. Follow these steps to connect your Instagram Business account with bitChat:

## Step 1: Ensure You Have an Instagram Business Account

To access business features on Instagram, you need to have a Business Account. If you haven't set one up yet, follow the guidelines provided by Instagram: [How to Create a Business Account](https://help.instagram.com/502981923235522).

## Step 2: Ensure You Have a Facebook Page

A Facebook Page is necessary to connect your Instagram Business account. If you don't have one yet, you can create one by following these steps: [How to Create a Facebook Page](https://www.facebook.com/help/104002523024878).

## Step 3: Link Your Instagram Business Account to Your Facebook Page

Connecting your Instagram Business account to your Facebook Page allows you to manage your communications in one place. To link your accounts:

1. Open Instagram and go to your profile.
2. Tap the menu icon (three horizontal lines) in the top-right corner.
3. Select **Settings** > **Account** > **Linked Accounts**.
4. Choose **Facebook** and log into your Facebook account.
5. Select the Facebook Page you want to link to your Instagram Business account.

## Step 4: Enable 'Allow Access to Messages' in Your Instagram Account

To allow bitChat to access your Instagram messages:

1. Open the Instagram app on your mobile device.
2. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Messages**.
3. Under **Message Controls**, enable **Allow Access to Messages**.

## Step 5: Connect to Instagram with bitChat

Now that your accounts are linked and message access is enabled, you can connect Instagram to bitChat:

1. Navigate to the [bitChat Integrations page](https://app.bitbybit.studio/integrations).
2. Click on **Instagram** and then click **Connect**.
3. Click on **Connect to Meta** to start the connection process.
4. Select the Instagram Business account you want to connect.
5. Choose the associated Facebook Page.
6. Review and grant the necessary permissions.
7. Click **Got it** to complete the connection.

Once connected, you can manage your Instagram messages directly from the bitChat dashboard.
