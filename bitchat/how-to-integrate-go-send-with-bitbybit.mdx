---
title: "How to Integrate BLITZ with bitbybit"
---

Easily connect BLITZ with bitbybit to unlock instant delivery options for your store. Because BLITZ requires a custom enterprise setup, our onboarding team provides a concierge service to handle the integration on your behalf. Follow the steps below to request your setup.

---

## Step 1: Open integrations menu & Locate BLITZ

- Log in to **bitbybit**.
- Navigate to the **Integrations** page from the side menu.
- Locate the **BLITZ** integration card.
- Click **Connect**. A modal will appear explaining the enterprise setup requirements.

## Step 2: Request Integration via WhatsApp

- Inside the modal, click the **Request Integration** button.
- You will be redirected to a new WhatsApp tab with a pre-filled message containing your Name and Company ID.
- Hit **Send** to deliver the message directly to the bitbybit onboarding team.

<img
  src="/images/Blitz_article_step2.png"
  alt="Blitz Article Step2"
  title="Blitz Article Step2"
  style={{ width:"46%" }}
  className="mx-auto"
/>

---

## Step 3: Wait for Enterprise Setup

- Our team will coordinate directly with BLITZ to securely generate your enterprise API keys.

⚠️ _Note: Please allow 7-14 business days for BLITZ to fully process and activate your corporate account._

---

## Step 4: Finalize the Connection

- Once your account is approved, the bitbybit team will notify you and unlock your configuration fields.
- Return to the **Integrations** page on bitbybit and click on **BLITZ**.
- The API fields will now be unlocked. The bitbybit team will either paste the credentials for you, or provide you with the keys to paste yourself.
- Click **Connect** to save the credentials.

You can now start dispatching BLITZ couriers directly from the bitbybit platform.
