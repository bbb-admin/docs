## Step 1: Go to WhatsApp API Profile settings.

1. Navigate to: [https://app.bitbybit.studio](https://app.bitbybit.studio)

## Step 2: Click "Upload image" on Profile photo.

1. In the Profile section, click the **"Upload image"** button.

## Step 3: Click "Save changes".

1. After uploading your image, click **"Save changes"** to apply the new profile picture.
