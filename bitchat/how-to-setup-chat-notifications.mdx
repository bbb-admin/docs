## **Step 1: Access Notification Settings**

- Go to [bitChat Settings - Notifications](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitchat/settings?section=notifications) dashboard.

## **Step 2: Enable Notification Preferences**

- Choose your preferred notification. You can choose multiple notifications.

- Customize the types of notifications you want to receive by toggling "Individual chat", "Ticket chat", "Group chat", "Assigned ticket (Pop-up)".
