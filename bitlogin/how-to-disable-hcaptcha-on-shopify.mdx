## Step 1: Log in to Your Shopify Admin Panel

- Access your Shopify admin panel.

## Step 2: Open Online Store Preferences

- Click **Online Store \> Preferences** from the left menu.

  ![Screenshot2025 09 01at16 53 29 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-09-01at16.53.29.png)

## Step 3:  Turn Off hCAPCTHA

- Scroll down to **Spam Protection.**
- Disable the toggle '**Enable on login, create account and password recovery pages.**'

  ![Screenshot2025 09 01at16 53 29copy Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-09-01at16.53.29copy.png)
- Click **Save**

  ![Screenshot2025 09 01at16 53 29copy2 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-09-01at16.53.29copy2.png)

By following these steps, you can ensure that bitLogin operates smoothly without interference from hCAPTCHA.
