## Step 4: Set Up Your Amazon OAuth

- Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret** provided.
- Go to the **bitLogin Amazon Settings** page.
- Paste the copied **Client ID** and **Client Secret** into the designated fields.

  ![Screenshot2025 08 20at15 47 34 Pn](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66b5f63da76cba9b9e8175de_AD_4nXde-YbcPmN0ym3JyoU4pArva1VbHA2gnjY3vnBotLgX2buPFGHJZf9rL4FrATdWAw-28-G-Zq5QrNDksnoo3rI2Xv3AdkthfmAGJfxP2bSuu8WlK1jd-GKTRAdYRZrBJvAN0YZqdvuIkMBwMOdoBiYo-2Fs.png)
- Click **Save Changes.**

  ![Screenshot2025 08 20at15 47 34 Pn](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66b5f63c56bcd584ec74b65f_AD_4nXfpAySHJl2xWbFzygqsp6Bcj36qWxNUSl8nx5YioERHnGePaTAj4GGtWG0hdjCgLwciDfU3D2EZbhnrZmI13vT9N6PV52GDJvZzobiUibjFfu1YIoeFAirrbJhqeMHSWXHPZVjZwIuSMr2I07lf9OSkPny0.png)
