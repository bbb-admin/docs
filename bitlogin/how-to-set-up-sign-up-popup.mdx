title: "How to Set Up Sign-Up Popup"
---

Encourage your customers to log in effortlessly with an automatic pop-up that appears 10 seconds after they enter your online store. This guide will walk you through the simple steps to set it up and enhance user engagement on your store!

## Step 1: Complete the Heading and Body Text

* Fill in the heading and body text.

![__wf_reserved_inherit](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67c6c3ae8292f601af6b2571_Untitled%20design%20(15).png)Fill in the fields**Note:** The heading text can have a maximum of 50 characters, and the body text can have up to 160 characters.

## Step 2: Customize Your Popup Appearance

* You can customize the popup's appearance, including the background theme, background color, layout options, font, and text color.

![__wf_reserved_inherit](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67c6c525e953e6e848dcfb6f_Screenshot%202025-03-04%20at%2016.16.08.png)Choose your background theme![__wf_reserved_inherit](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67c6c53c5028406415341d06_Screenshot%202025-03-04%20at%2016.16.20.png)Choose your popup layout![__wf_reserved_inherit](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67c6c5527cac31fe1421a4a8_Screenshot%202025-03-04%20at%2016.16.27.png)Choose your font style* Additionally, you can select the social login options to enable (minimum 1, maximum 2) and configure the button theme, button style, and button size.

![__wf_reserved_inherit](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67c6c56984aae716252ff0a6_Screenshot%202025-03-04%20at%2016.16.51.png)Choose your social login button![__wf_reserved_inherit](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67c6c59f0fcdabe85dca3bd8_Screenshot%202025-03-04%20at%2016.16.41.png)Choose your button style## Step 3: Preview and Confirm Your Popup

* Once you have completed the setup, you can preview how the popup will appear on your store from the right side of the screen. Adjust the design as needed to match your preferences.

![__wf_reserved_inherit](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67c6c6b2e953e6e848de6283_Screenshot%202025-03-04%20at%2016.23.26.png)Popup live preview**Note:** You can switch between mobile and desktop views.

## Step 4: Enable the Sign-Up Popup

* To activate the popup, simply toggle the switch until it turns green.
