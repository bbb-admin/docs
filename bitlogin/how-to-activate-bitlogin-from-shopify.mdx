# How to Activate bitLogin from Shopify

Integrate bitLogin with your Shopify store to offer customers secure and convenient login options, enhancing user experience and streamlining account management.

## Step 1: Enable "Legacy" Customer Accounts in Shopify

1. Log in to your Shopify dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Settings** > **Customer accounts**.
3. Select **"Legacy"** to enable legacy customer accounts.

<img
  src="/images/enable-legacy-customer-accounts.png"
  alt="Enable Legacy Customer Accounts"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 2: Activate bitLogin in Your Shopify Theme

1. Go to **Online Store** > **Themes**.
2. Click on **Customize** for your active theme.
3. In the theme editor, navigate to **App embeds**.
4. Toggle on **"bitLogin Social Login"** and click **"Save"**.

<img
  src="/images/activate-bitlogin-theme.png"
  alt="Activate bitLogin in Shopify Theme"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 3: Disable hCaptcha in Shopify Admin

To ensure bitLogin functions correctly, disable hCaptcha:

1. In your Shopify admin, go to **Settings** > **Apps and sales channels** > **Online Store** > **Preferences**.
2. Scroll down to the **Spam Protection** section.
3. Turn off **hCaptcha**.

For detailed instructions, refer to [How to Disable hCAPTCHA on Shopify](https://www.bitbybit.studio/knowledge-base/how-to-turn-off-hcaptcha-on-shopify).

## Step 4: Verify bitLogin Integration

1. Return to the bitLogin dashboard.
2. Click on **"Preview"** at the bottom right corner.
3. Ensure the social login buttons are displayed correctly on your store's login page.

<img
  src="/images/verify-bitlogin-integration.png"
  alt="Verify bitLogin Integration"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>
