# How to Set Up Facebook OAuth

Integrating Facebook login into your application allows users to authenticate using their Facebook credentials, providing a seamless and secure login experience. Follow these steps to set up Facebook OAuth with bitLogin.

## Step 1: Create a Facebook Developer Account

- Visit the [Facebook for Developers](https://developers.facebook.com) website.
- Click on **"Get Started"** or log in with your existing Facebook credentials.

<img
  src="/images/facebook-developer-login.png"
  alt="Facebook Developer Login"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 2: Create a New App

- Navigate to the [Facebook Apps](https://developers.facebook.com/apps/) page.
- Click on **"Create App"**.
- Select **"Consumer"** as the app type.
- Enter your app name, contact email, and choose a Business Account.
- Click **"Create App"**.

<img
  src="/images/create-facebook-app.png"
  alt="Create Facebook App"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 3: Configure Use Case

- In the left sidebar, click on **"Use Case"**.
- Under **"Authentication and Account Creation"**, click **"Customize"**.
- In the permissions section, click **"Add"** for **email** and wait for its status to update to **"Ready for testing"**.
- Click **"Go To Settings"**.
- In the **"Valid OAuth Redirect URIs"** section, input:
  `https://api.bitbybit.studio/bitlogin/api/login/facebook/callback`
- Click **"Save Changes"** and return to the dashboard.

<img
  src="/images/facebook-use-case.png"
  alt="Facebook Use Case Configuration"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 4: App Settings Configuration

- Navigate to **"App Settings"** > **"Basic"**.
- Fill in the required details:
- **Display Name**: Enter your store's name.
- **Namespace**: Optional.
- **App Domains**: Enter your store’s domain.
- **Privacy Policy URL**: Use your store’s privacy policy URL or [https://bitbybit.studio/privacy](https://bitbybit.studio/privacy).
- **Terms of Service URL**: Use your store’s terms URL or [https://www.bitbybit.studio/terms-conditions](https://www.bitbybit.studio/terms-conditions).
- **App Icon**: Upload your store’s logo.
- **Category**: Select "Utility & Productivity".
- **User Data Deletion**: Enter [https://www.bitbybit.studio/terms-conditions](https://www.bitbybit.studio/terms-conditions).
- Click **"Save Changes"**.

<img
  src="/images/facebook-app-settings.png"
  alt="Facebook App Settings"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 5: Configure Facebook OAuth in bitLogin

- Copy your **"App ID"** and **"App Secret"**.
- Go to the [bitLogin Facebook Settings](https://app.bitbybit.studio) page.
- Paste your **"App ID"** and **"App Secret"** into the respective fields.
- Click **"Save Changes"**.
- Enable the **"Use your store name on Facebook login"** toggle until it turns green.

<img
  src="/images/bitlogin-facebook-setup.png"
  alt="Configure Facebook OAuth in bitLogin"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 6: Publish Your Facebook App

- Go to the **"Go Live"** section.
- Verify your business, then select your verified business.
- Answer the required questions in the **"Data Handling Questions"** section.
- Click **"Go Live"** to make the app publicly accessible for Facebook login.
- Once published, the Facebook login will be ready to use in your store.

<img
  src="/images/facebook-app-publish.png"
  alt="Publish Facebook App"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

## Step 7: Completion

Congratulations! You've successfully set up Facebook OAuth and obtained your App ID and App Secret. Make sure to store your app secret securely, as it’s crucial for authentication.
