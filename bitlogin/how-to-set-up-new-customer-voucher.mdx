This exclusive voucher is only available to users who have just registered. This is a special offer that returning users won't see, making it a unique advantage for first-time signups.

## Step 1: Create Your Voucher

* There are 3 available discount types: Fixed Amount, Percentage, and Free Shipping. Please select one and enter the required fields, such as Voucher Title, Prefix, Suffix, Value, Minimum Spend, and Active Voucher Dates.

![__wf_reserved_inherit](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67c806be6bab46d0abed11b5_guide-voucher-2.png)Click "Create voucher"## Step 2: Complete the Heading and Body Text

* Fill in the heading and body text.
