---
title: "How to Push Out of Stock Products to the Bottom of Collections"
---

Optimize your product collections by automatically moving out-of-stock items to the bottom, keeping the focus on available products. This guide explains how to configure this feature in bitAI for a better shopping experience.

## **Step 1: Access Sorting Dashboard**

* Go to [bitAI - Sorting](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitai/sorting) dashboard.

## **Step 2: Select and Enable OOS Collection**

* Choose the desired product collection and enable “OOS” toggle.
