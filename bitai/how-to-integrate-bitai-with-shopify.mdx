---
title: "How to Integrate bitAI with Shopify"
---

Integrate bitAI with Shopify to streamline data sharing and access AI-driven insights for your store. This connection allows for seamless management and improved analytics across platforms. Here’s a quick guide to setting up the bitAI and Shopify integration.

## **Step 1: Access Integrations Dashboard**

- Go to the [Integrations](https://app.bitbybit.studio/integrations) dashboard and click "Connect".

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66f23910dff711e73091cd64_AD_4nXdjCHbqxUPFeBPgC0Y-tqwz9eJtCMLz8v4X95WX-AOJshPJoT6fwSbNfQKjONZd3m5kIGJswpd6TNV-Z3mXPZUeT2dU7pUVlAHoyang33D-g60NV1QGxJS5XTeIaz4JcOEKT8xiOlZXTXVuKxTPSrVi2usy.png)

Click "Connect"

## **Step 2: Connect to Shopify**

- Click "Connect to Shopify" and click “Install”.

Click "Connect to Shopify"

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/66f236d93ddda8c3c98350c1_AD_4nXdtbczJqZQ5igzC0gcUowLG4LQlR7U9VTTLYRDAmea7QtVI5fkpqQapVJ3v_4pyUm8KnTUtpTAhnBpAtzBLWzfvahRAR822gk_2vEmARyhcX1OWXjSdtlRsEdGDwOAJ0ksYKSL7dTDJrY0sSd_3q3-qrZ_x.png)

##  **Step 3: Choose Your Shopify Store**

- Select the company or "Create new companny" or "Login to another account".

## **Step 4: Verify Integration**

- If you have successfully entered the bitAI dashboard, your Shopify is already integrated with bitAI.
