---
title: "How to Create a Sorting Model"
---

Organize your e-commerce products more effectively by creating a sorting model in bitAI. This tutorial guides you through building a model that customizes product ordering, ensuring a more intuitive shopping experience for your customers.

## **Step 1: Access Model Dashboard**

* Go to the [bitAI Model](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitai/model) dashboard.

## **Step 2: Create a New Model**

* Click "Create Model".

* Enter a name for your new model.
