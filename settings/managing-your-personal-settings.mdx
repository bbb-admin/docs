title: "Managing Your Personal Settings"
---

Customize your user profile in bitbybit to reflect your current information, including profile images, display names, and contact numbers. This tutorial ensures you can manage your personal settings effortlessly.

## **Step 1: Access Account Settings**

* Go to [Settings - Account](https://app.bitbybit.studio/settings) dashboard.

## **Step 2: Update Profile Image**

* Click "Replace" and upload a new image.
