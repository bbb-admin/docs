---
title: "How to Top Up AI Token"
---

Maintain access to advanced AI features by topping up your AI tokens through the bitAI dashboard. This guide will walk you through selecting token amounts, payment methods, and completing the purchase for uninterrupted service.

## **Step 1: Access Plan Management**

- Go to [Settings - Plan management](https://app.bitbybit.studio/settings?section=plan-management) dashboard.

## **Step 2: Start AI Token Top-Up**

- Click "Top-up AI Token."

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67120ec7bf13639d2ea3da01_AD_4nXf68YsHmc9rUSAPcx3SrBDqztNddx1Buox5F94LwU7dvwk-AO01J-WRGlFSRLtwCebKwQsopY8MRzzvGjfzZBUcqU0fwwCAzHJyzGn7Z89phTCwX2n4ekZ61woI8I6SGGsy-3HjrWtQnhP3LVudfYno8QUO.png)

Click "Top-up AI Token"## **Step 3: Choose Token Amount**

- Select the amount of AI tokens you wish to purchase and click "Continue to payment."

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67120ec83296341cf80b50fe_AD_4nXfN7GFMFz0Td9bn3mRRVYK97r0Q0nlha9-LCyNS48M2_QN3ytEzhHaLhAooun-jWxgLG_bFct6W4YCC_PWBfipuLwPhTCQ7If6XRTOg_02akGIiYF3k42UP_U2wLJ58w-X6s4cjxwfyekIMX04oLorjRvSX.png)

Select the amount of AI tokens and click "Continue to payment"

## **Step 4: Choose Payment Method**

- You’ll be directed to Shopify then choose your payment method or add a new credit card.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67120ec8cc05a8c4956472cf_AD_4nXcHMNCF3_w8A4fUCeYXrOcCwq-fncuAtIOckc2aQp22gmS4L5Tet-OZglMgC7SXmbEGEi5vGqyw-B-7eitQy0RLiuqXLZdAVFHPUR98s5Fbb2yYohsc1ch6sNFc1SLopJPzzfdd_oU6EBNtBsZg4_SZwuXQ.png)

Choose your payment method or add a new credit card

## **Step 5: Complete Payment**

- Click “Approve”.
