title: "How To Get Company ID Article"
---

If you need to locate your unique Company ID for support or integration purposes, follow these simple steps within your bitbybit dashboard.

## Step 1: Go to Settings

Log in to your bitbybit account. On the side panel menu, locate and click on the **Settings** icon.

![Image](/images/image.webp)

## Step 2: Access Company Settings

Once you are on the Settings page, look for the **General** section in the menu. Click on **Company** to open your company profile details.

![Image(6)](/images/image\(6\).png)

## Step 3: Locate the ID

On the Company settings page, scroll down to the very bottom of the screen. You will find your specific **Company ID** displayed there.

![Image(7)](/images/image\(7\).png)
