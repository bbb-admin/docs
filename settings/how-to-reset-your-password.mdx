---
title: "How to Reset Your Password"
---

Learn how to securely reset your password in just a few steps. This tutorial is essential for maintaining account security and regaining access if you’ve forgotten your login credentials. Here’s how to get it done quickly.

## **If You Haven’t Logged In:**

### **Step 1: Access Login Page**

- On the [Login](https://app.bitbybit.studio/login) page, click “Forgot password”

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/672082c8c9da64a99e822fc7_AD_4nXeaMmJSW1F85_otkqubGebujyc1xY3Vs-Hfwl85rJhIaGofwIB1j1zNfAaudr2A4jhKMjPu2ekt14NugwLLJ-L_rCsp-N_xtk_rI9IhSWwlH3ynRM1tfC51f03vALVbs3OaYAkY_-IpSWaGCAuwW_yhqrU.png)

Click "Forgot password"

## **Step 2: Send Email to Reset**

- Enter your email and click “Send email”

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/672082c8fb6001628b778a05_AD_4nXeHLxm6b_xsJjd0RC0aAoezvANPMN-inmSAu3VDtZCSxccCpi_IeSagurn6kA2k4J3LJYopLJvQkIel0l3_GQ0yLX_sr_ycI8kYOD9w2JWt06Jd_W-jcG4SQEPjrcWTfyGxwihserYzD6O4DP469ifJT8NM.png)

Enter "Email" and click “Send email”

## **Step 3: Check Your Email**

- Open the password reset email and click "Reset My Password."

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67120cb1d5eb1895e4b2cc4e_AD_4nXf4_JZeQbKtp8V4dQ0aDjg4VXlc7cDtTUUbYu6O3f1AQssQModzdu_ZobfSLXPd91wo7UqDlXxCtIzLc_fd6Q43VWgwVZoQndHrZhQD0C5k9YnpGalEqC5-ZJikH7axbBObuOmjpv_j53MBMD6QFinKwHGw.png)

Click "Reset My Password"

### **Step 4: Set a New Password**

- Enter a new password that meets the requirements. Then click “Submit”.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67120cb223cf2e9f90d338a1_AD_4nXfMxpzi_MsaL3fPAO5yW97DBwqxKaquURsuBjxwj5yy9d0TuoeSkQHUl68UfKu6QVVebrknDiZUSDKHBbueRk-rQza5slalban57Uo1sKimEbJBDeRkVkgmn4k2Bc7hOiPozDTGm6KAs1YSp0W1hIjEkdBP.png)

Enter a new password then click “Submit”

## **If You're Already Logged In:**

### **Step 1: Access Account Settings**

- Go to [Settings - Account](https://app.bitbybit.studio/settings?section=account) dashboard.

### **Step 2: Initiate Password Change**

- Click "Change password."

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67120cb223cf2e9f90d3389e_AD_4nXfnHLt8hJBbfmdr4fHFoyyddD0fKmDdJS2lcEd3NanrIaEhkPmze-KlOODPlL0FXyXPBMUYicRZNHmB2iKafxxkvGepcxmkMzgkUw-Sl8mprQ1JClvIYGr5V_hM8N7qR_IdeQcxwjjyl1qM-Z1BQS8vCw0.png)

Click "Change password"

### **Step 3: Check Your Email**

- Open the password reset email and click "Reset My Password."

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67120cb1d5eb1895e4b2cc4e_AD_4nXf4_JZeQbKtp8V4dQ0aDjg4VXlc7cDtTUUbYu6O3f1AQssQModzdu_ZobfSLXPd91wo7UqDlXxCtIzLc_fd6Q43VWgwVZoQndHrZhQD0C5k9YnpGalEqC5-ZJikH7axbBObuOmjpv_j53MBMD6QFinKwHGw.png)

Click "Reset My Password"

### **Step 4: Set a New Password**

- Enter a new password that meets the requirements. Then click “Submit”.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67120cb223cf2e9f90d338a1_AD_4nXfMxpzi_MsaL3fPAO5yW97DBwqxKaquURsuBjxwj5yy9d0TuoeSkQHUl68UfKu6QVVebrknDiZUSDKHBbueRk-rQza5slalban57Uo1sKimEbJBDeRkVkgmn4k2Bc7hOiPozDTGm6KAs1YSp0W1hIjEkdBP.png)
