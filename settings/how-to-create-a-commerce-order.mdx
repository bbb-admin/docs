If you are ready to streamline your sales process by creating orders manually and managing them directly through bitbybit, here is a guide on how to use the Commerce feature. Please remember to check the Requirements section so you can follow the process smoothly.

## Requirements Before You Start the Process

- **Active bitbybit Account** You must have an active account with access to the Commerce features.
- **Connected Store** Your e-commerce store (e.g., Shopify) must be properly integrated with bitbybit.
- **Product Catalog** Your products must be synced and available in the bitbybit dashboard.
- **Staff Permissions** Ensure your user account has permissions to "Create Orders" and "Manage Orders."

## Create and Manage Orders Process

**Step 1: Access the Commerce Dashboard**

1. **Log in** to your bitbybit account.
2. **Navigate** to the **Commerce** or **Orders** tab in the main sidebar menu.

**Step 2: Create a New Order**

1. **Click** the **"Create Order"** (or "New Order") button, typically located at the top right of the Orders dashboard.
2. **Select Customer**: Search for an existing customer by name/phone number or create a new customer profile.
3. **Add Products**: Browse or search your catalog to add items to the cart. You can adjust quantities and select variants here.

⚠️ **Note:** Ensure you select the correct variants (size, color) to keep your inventory accurate.

**Step 3: Review and Finalize Order**

1. **Review Cart**: Check the total amount, including any applied taxes or shipping fees.
2. **Set Status**: Choose the initial status of the order (e.g., "Pending Payment" or "Draft").
3. **Send Payment Link (Optional)**: If the customer needs to pay, you can generate a payment link and send it directly via WhatsApp.

**Step 4: Manage Existing Orders**

1. Return to the **Orders Dashboard** to view a list of all current orders.
2. **Click** on an order ID to view its details.
3. **Update Status**: You can change the status from "Pending" to "Paid," "Fulfilled," or "Cancelled" as the order progresses.

**Step 5: Order Fulfillment**

1. Once an order is paid, click **"Fulfill Item"** to initiate the shipping process.
2. Enter the tracking information if available.
3. The customer will receive an update regarding their order status.

**Congratulations! You have successfully created and managed an order using the bitbybit Commerce feature.** This tool allows you to handle manual orders and customer requests efficiently, ensuring a smooth shopping experience.

Happy selling!
