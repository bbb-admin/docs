---
title: "How to Add a Mailing List Button"
---

Increase your subscriber count by adding a mailing list button to your bitLink page. This button makes it easy for visitors to join your newsletter directly from your social media link page. Follow these simple steps to set it up.

## **Step 1: Access Your bitLink Dashboard**

* Go to your [bitLink Subscription](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitlink/subscription) dashboard.

## **Step 2: Activate & Choose Style Your Mailing List**

* Enable the "Email" toggle.
