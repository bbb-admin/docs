---
title: "How to Customize Your Link Page's Theme"
---

Make your bitLink page stand out by customizing its theme to align with your brand. Adjust background, fonts, and colors for a personalized look that resonates with your audience. Follow this guide for a quick and effective setup.

## **Step 1: Access the Appearance Dashboard**

* Go to the [bitLink Appearance](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitlink/appearance) dashboard.

## **Step 2: Choose a Theme Preset**

* Select your desired preset theme for quick customization.

* Select “Solid color”, “Gradient” or click “Upload Image” to upload your own preferences and adjust the image opacity.

* Choose button style.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67120b2a884bcc699b649ceb_AD_4nXc56D_WxWVwyJcWAGEINk7vCbEomkR6osfF0p1I2nyFVvv-BrX2JhkiQoyAPUw7mNqqQpX8tXJ-sA1bGGieV8uKk8k2S75bnu0VKjh_g_5K0BGOmE-YToYFQWoc1tBUjNaIjegm5O2E1Tt0Tr1dRFsUeDU.png)Choose button style## **Step 6: Set Fonts and Colors**

* Choose the font dropdown to select text styles and colors.  


![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/659655f5b20d395a5c69efcf/67120b2a98520d4adef3b491_AD_4nXdo6W5O4imW04KvJ09BsJlAVoFvbLU7mcTGofA0vPWvO1U6q3xMTD-hHEomch84a8RELHPwkLI-DoIRwA0BjueVEcXtWDBShs0ATQs0RZ6arLYFbta41P2OTFBNYEweHzMiZ-AIRol8E-LhkN5PPs5TQmTz.png)Choose text styles and text colors## **Step 7: Hide Branding**

* You can hide the bitbybit logo for a cleaner look. However, this feature is only available for Standard Plan. If you wish to have this feature, you can upgrade your plan.

## **Step 8: Preview Changes**

* Preview your customized page on the right side of the screen.
