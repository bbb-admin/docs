---
title: "How to Archive a Link"
---

Keep your bitLink page organized without deleting links by archiving them. Archiving temporarily hides links from your profile while retaining them for future use. Here’s how to archive and restore links with a simple toggle.

## **Step 1: Access bitLink Dashboard**

* Go to [bitLink Links](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitlink/links) and select the “Links” you want to archive.

## **Step 2: Toggle the Link Off**

* Click the "Green Toggle" to switch it to "Gray," effectively hiding the link from your bitLink.
