title: "How to Edit Your Link URL"
---

Customizing your bitLink URL makes it easier for customers to remember and access your page. Editing your link URL is a quick way to create a more professional appearance. Here’s how you can modify your URL in a few simple steps.

## **Step 1:** Access the bitLink Links Dashboard

* Go to [bitLink Links Dashboard](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitlink/links)**.**

## **Step 2:** Customize Your URL

* Click “URL” and edit your "Brand Name".
