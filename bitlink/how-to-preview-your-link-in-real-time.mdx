---
title: "How to Preview Your Link in Real Time"
---

Previewing your bitLink page in real time ensures that all the elements are in place before you publish. This feature allows you to see how your page looks on both desktop and mobile, providing a complete view of your customer’s experience. Follow this guide to learn how to preview your bitLink page effectively.

## **Step 1:** Access Your [bitLink Dashboard](https://app.bitbybit.studio/bitlink/links)

* After filling in the required sections, click “See Preview” to check how your page looks.
