## Part A: System Prompt

The system prompt defines who your AI is and how it behaves in every conversation.

### A1. Agent Identity

<Accordion title="What to fill in">
**Agent Name** ‚Äî The name your AI uses when greeting customers. Keep it short and memorable. Examples: "Adi", "Rasa", "Maya"

**Brand Name** ‚Äî Your business name as customers know it.

**Brand Description** ‚Äî Explain what you sell and who you serve. This helps your AI understand your business context.

*Example:*
> "adidas Indonesia is a sportswear brand selling athletic footwear, apparel, and accessories to athletes and lifestyle customers who value performance and style."
</Accordion>

### A2. Conversation Style

<Accordion title="What to fill in">
**Primary Language** ‚Äî The language your AI starts conversations in. It will automatically switch if customers respond in a different language.

**Tone of Voice** ‚Äî How should your AI sound?
- **Professional & Helpful** ‚Äî Best for B2B, electronics, services
- **Friendly & Casual** ‚Äî Best for fashion, lifestyle, retail
- **Energetic & Fun** ‚Äî Best for sports, entertainment, F&B
- **Warm & Caring** ‚Äî Best for beauty, wellness, kids products

**Personality Details** ‚Äî Add specific phrases, emoji preferences, or language quirks.

*Example:*
> "Address customers as 'Kak'. Use food emojis naturally üçîüçü. Keep responses short (2-3 sentences)."
</Accordion>

### A3. Welcome Message

<Accordion title="What to fill in">
This is the first message customers see when they start a conversation. A great welcome message has three parts:

1. **Greeting** ‚Äî Warm, branded hello
2. **Value** ‚Äî What you offer or what's popular
3. **Action** ‚Äî Clear next step

*Example:*
> "Halo Kak! üëã Selamat datang di McDonald's!
>
> Mau pesan Big Mac, McChicken, atau menu lainnya? Langsung aja, aku bantu!"
>
> [Button: Lihat Menu]

**Button Text** ‚Äî The call-to-action button shown with your welcome message. Examples: "Lihat Menu", "Lihat Katalog", "Mulai Belanja"
</Accordion>

### A4. Business Rules (Optional)

<Accordion title="What to fill in">
Add critical rules your AI should always enforce. Keep this focused ‚Äî detailed policies go in the Knowledge Base.

**Delivery & Orders** ‚Äî Minimum orders, delivery fees, estimated times
> "Free shipping for orders above Rp 500,000. Standard delivery 3-5 business days."

**Product Guarantees** ‚Äî Quality promises, certifications
> "All products 100% authentic. 30-day return for unworn items with tags."

**Important Restrictions** ‚Äî Things your AI should never promise
> "Never guarantee stock availability without checking. Cannot combine promo codes."
</Accordion>

### A5. Escalation Triggers (Optional)

<Accordion title="What to fill in">
List keywords or situations that should transfer the conversation to a human agent.

*Examples:*
- Quality issues: "defective, broken, wrong item, fake"
- Order problems: "refund, return, cancel, missing"
- Direct requests: "talk to human, speak to agent"
- Complex needs: "bulk order, corporate, partnership"
</Accordion>

### A6. Forbidden Actions (Optional)

<Accordion title="What to fill in">
Tell your AI what it should never do.

*Examples:*
- "Never promise exact delivery dates"
- "Never offer unauthorized discounts"
- "Never mention competitor brands"
- "Never guarantee specific promotional items"
</Accordion>

### A7. Upselling (Optional)

<Accordion title="What to fill in">
Define when and how your AI should suggest additional items. Good upselling feels helpful, not pushy.

**Structure your upsell:**
1. **When** ‚Äî The trigger situation
2. **Suggest** ‚Äî What to recommend
3. **Script** ‚Äî Exact words to use

*Example:*
> When: Customer orders burger only (no meal)
> Suggest: Upgrade to meal package
> Script: "Kak, mending jadi paket aja! Tambah Rp 15,000 udah dapat fries + drink."
</Accordion>

### A8. Checkout (Optional)

<Accordion title="What to fill in">
**Customer Selections** ‚Äî What customers need to specify before ordering. Examples: Size, Color, Quantity, Spice Level, Add-ons

**Payment Methods** ‚Äî Check all the payment methods you accept:
- Bank Transfer (BCA, Mandiri, BNI, etc.)
- E-wallet (ShopeePay, OVO, DANA, GoPay)
- QRIS
- Credit/Debit Card
</Accordion>

## What's required vs optional

<Info>
**Only 4 fields are required** to launch your AI. Everything else can be added later.
</Info>

### Required to launch

| Field | Why it's needed |
|-------|-----------------|
| Agent Name | Your AI needs a name to introduce itself |
| Brand Name | Identifies your business in conversations |
| Brand Description | Helps AI understand what you sell |
| Welcome Message | First impression for every customer |

### Optional (add anytime)

All other fields enhance your AI but aren't required to go live:
- Conversation style preferences
- Business rules and restrictions
- Escalation triggers
- Upselling scripts
- Knowledge Base content

Start simple. Launch with the basics and add more as you learn what your customers ask.
